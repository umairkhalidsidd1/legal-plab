Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2, 2026

Welcome to PLAB Study App. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide you with the best study experience:

  • Study Progress: Your quiz scores, study progress, and bookmarks are stored locally on your device.
  • Device Information: Basic device information for app functionality and crash reporting.
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous usage data to improve app performance and features.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Track your study progress and provide personalized recommendations
  • Improve app functionality and user experience
  • Fix bugs and improve app stability
  • Provide customer support when requested

3. Data Storage

Your study progress and preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not store your personal study data on external servers. This means your data stays with you and is not shared with third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

Our app may use third-party services that have their own privacy policies:

  • Apple App Store: For app distribution and in-app purchases
  • RevenueCat: For subscription management (if applicable)
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics to improve the app

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. Since most data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data also depends on your device's security settings.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for medical professionals and students. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years of age.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your study data stored in the app
  • Delete your progress data through the app settings
  • Request information about how your data is used

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes through the app or by updating the "Last updated" date.
